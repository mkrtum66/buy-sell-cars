How to sell my car in Perth

With Car Buyers it’s easy, we provide online and over the phone offers to buy your car, van and 4x4’s. As long as your car was built from 2007 onwards we’re interested in making a deal with you.

Once you accept our offer, you’ll have the cash within 24 hours. Once you receive the money, only then we’ll organise pickup of your vehicle so you’ll always have peace of mind.

We come to you – at home or at work

Our Perth team at Car Buyers come to your home or work to inspect your vehicle. We operate 7 days a week so finding a convenient time that works for you is no issue for our team.

We buy cars, as is!

No need for costly mechanic bills, we’ll buy your car as is with no Road Worthy Certificate required. Save yourself the time, stress and inconvenience of having to organise the paperwork, we’ll do it for you!

How to add value to my car

We offer fair, realistic prices; however if you’re looking to increase the value of your car, please keep the following in mind:

  • If you owe finance on your vehicle, please provide us with an up-to-date payout letter
  • Driver’s license and registration will help speed up the process
  • Service history books, spare keys and any receipts from major repairs
